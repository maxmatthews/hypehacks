Bring the Hype.

What is HypeHacks?

HypeHacks is a student run 48-hour hackathon created to hype up the hackathons we know and love, as well as to honor Tristan Wiley’s birthday. It is scheduled to begin on September 2nd, 11am EST, and finish on September 4th, 1pm EST. We plan to bring hundreds of hackers, ranging from students around the globe and beyond, to create some innovative projects. Can you bring the Hype?

Frequently asked questions

Can I Attend?

Definitely :) Anyone is allowed to hack.

How Much Does This Cost?

Nothing my fine friend! This event is absolutely free for all! Just come with a great idea and creativity.

What if I don’t have a team?

If you don’t have a team now, that’s okay! We’ll have a Slack channel available on the day for all participants to share ideas and form teams.

What about hardware hacks?

Since this hackathon is online and not in person we will not be able to supply hardware. If you end up building a hardware hack you must record a demo of your hack to submit with your project. Don’t forget to put your code on Github!

What’s the schedule?

We’ll release a more detailed schedule mid-week before the event. For now, know that we’re planning to start hacking Friday at 12 am and have a closing ceremony on Sunday 1pm.

How Does Registration Work?

Registration will be opening on August 19th and will remain open till the day of the hackathon! We’ll be sending out follow-up emails prior to and on the day of the event to keep you up to speed.

Where is this Online?

For HypeHacks we will have several specific main chat locations that will be on platforms such as Skype, Slack, and Google hangouts. However, you are welcome to communicate on any platform you can think of as long as you have the ability to upload you project to GitHub and submit your project on Devpost. We’ll send out an email to all accepted applicants.

What will I Need?

All you need is a computer, internet connection, red bull and a hyped up idea.

How do teams work?

Teams are 5 people at max. You’re allowed to come in with your own team as long as you’re all registered for the event!
